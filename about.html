About the Artist

My Story

Crafting Memories with Every Stitch

Hello! I'm the creator behind Heartfelt Handmade. My journey began with a simple love for sewing and a desire to preserve cherished memories.

When my grandmother passed away, I wanted something tangible to remember her by—something softer than a photograph. I crafted my first memorial bear from her favorite cardigan, and the comfort it brought was indescribable.

Since then, I have been honored to create hundreds of dolls for families across Canada, turning treasured clothing into huggable heirlooms.
