About Gnome Memory Bear Order
Meet the Maker

Sports, Storytelling & Soulful Keepsakes

Hi! I’m Hanna Landry in Dieppe, New Brunswick-based maker with a passion for two things: sports and storytelling.

I started making my custom sports gnomes because every fan needs a little extra luck on their side!

But my work took on a new meaning after the passing of my father-in-law. Creating Memory Bears and Pillows has become a way for me to help others heal while fulfilling my own heart.

Whether you are looking for a unique gift for a sports lover or a soulful keepsake to honor a memory, I’m here to craft something special just for you.
